Puppy Chow

Description

Chex mix covered in chocolate and powdered sugar

Ingredients

  • Chex Mix
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Peanut Butter
  • Butter
  • Butterscotch Chips

    • Steps

    1. Melt chocolate, peanut butter, and butterscotch chips
    2. Pour over chex mix
    3. Coat with powdered sugar
    4. Enjoy
